swe-edge-ingress-tls — server-side TLS and mTLS for inbound servers.

Provides IngressTlsConfig (loaded from PEM files) and TlsSvc::build_tls_acceptor which constructs a tokio_rustls::TlsAcceptor backed by the ring CryptoProvider without requiring a process-wide default.
